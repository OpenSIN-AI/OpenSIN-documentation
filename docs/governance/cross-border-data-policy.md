# Cross Border Data Policy

> **Version:** 1.0 | **Date:** 2026-04-04 | **Owner:** Security Team | **Status:** ✅ Active

## Purpose

This policy establishes the guidelines and requirements for cross border data policy within the OpenSIN-AI ecosystem.

## Scope

This policy applies to:
- All OpenSIN-AI repositories and services
- All team members and contributors
- All third-party integrations and dependencies
- All data processed, stored, or transmitted by OpenSIN-AI

## Policy Statement

Cross Border Data Policy must be implemented according to the following requirements:

### Requirements

1. **Requirement 1:** All systems must implement cross border data policy controls
2. **Requirement 2:** Regular audits must be conducted to verify compliance
3. **Requirement 3:** Non-compliance must be reported and remediated within 30 days
4. **Requirement 4:** All team members must complete training on this policy annually

### Implementation Guidelines

| Aspect | Requirement | Verification |
|--------|-------------|--------------|
| **Technical** | Automated controls | Automated testing |
| **Process** | Documented procedures | Regular audits |
| **People** | Trained staff | Annual training |
| **Monitoring** | Continuous monitoring | Real-time alerts |

## Roles and Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
|------|----------------|
| **Security Team** | Policy creation and enforcement |
| **Engineering Team** | Implementation and maintenance |
| **Ops Team** | Monitoring and incident response |
| **Management** | Policy approval and resource allocation |

## Compliance

Compliance with this policy is mandatory. Violations may result in:
- Immediate remediation requirements
- Temporary access restrictions
- Escalation to management
- Permanent access revocation

## Review

This policy is reviewed annually or when significant changes occur.
